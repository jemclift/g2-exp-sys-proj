ECOBYTE

Ecobyte is a sustainability app that we have developed to specifically focus on environmental sustainability.
This specific section of sustainability means finding a balance that allows for the consumption of resources to match
that of replenishment of these same resources. This is a practice that is very difficult to find and even more difficult to keep.
There are many actions that people can take to improve our levels of sustainability. Our app would hope to make these choices
easier to find out about and monitor. Once those choices are found and we know what actions are sustainable, it can become
difficult to stick to this regime. There is no sense of reward for individuals and thus no incentive to continue making these sustainable choices.
We would like to introduce a solution to this problem by rewarding people for making sustainable choices and rewarding them for continuing to
make these choices. The overarching aim of our project is to promote, develop and encourage sustainable lifestyles of any extent.
We would like to provide a sense of community recognition and reward on an individual level which would aim to improve sustainable practices.
